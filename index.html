Features

Everything you need in an AI chat client

Built for power users who want full control over their AI experience.

🧠

GPT-5.4 & GPT-5.4 Pro

Access the latest OpenAI models with full support for reasoning effort levels — from none to xhigh — giving you precise control over response depth.

✨

iOS 26 Liquid Glass

Native liquid glass effects throughout the interface. Frosted panels, translucent bubbles, and depth-aware layering that feels like a first-party Apple app.

📷

Multimodal Input

Send images alongside your messages. Supports HEIC, JPEG, PNG, and WebP with automatic format conversion for seamless iPhone integration.

📐

LaTeX & Math Rendering

Beautiful inline and block math formula rendering. Supports standard LaTeX delimiters including $$, \[...\], and \(...\) notation.

⚡

Real-time Streaming

Responses stream in real-time with automatic reconnection. 45-second stall detection with up to 3 retries ensures you never lose a response.

🔐

Your Key, Your Privacy

Your OpenAI API key is stored securely on your device and sent directly to OpenAI. No intermediary servers, no data collection, no tracking.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Visit platform.openai.com/api-keys to create an API key. You will need an OpenAI account with billing enabled. Once created, paste the key into the Settings tab of the app.
The app supports GPT-5.4 and GPT-5.4 Pro. GPT-5.4 offers reasoning effort levels from none to xhigh, while GPT-5.4 Pro supports medium, high, and xhigh reasoning. The default is GPT-5.4 Pro with xhigh reasoning for the most thorough responses.
Reasoning effort controls how much "thinking" the model does before responding. Higher effort (like xhigh) produces more thorough, carefully considered answers but uses more tokens. Lower effort (like none or low) gives faster, more concise responses. You can see the model's reasoning process in a collapsible section above each response.
Yes. On iOS, your API key is stored using the device's Secure Enclave via Expo SecureStore. It never leaves your device except when sent directly to OpenAI's API. There are no intermediary servers — your requests go straight from your device to OpenAI.
Yes. Tap the image button in the input bar to select photos from your library. The app automatically converts iPhone HEIC photos to JPEG format before sending them to OpenAI's multimodal API. You can attach images alongside text messages.
The app has built-in auto-reconnection. If the stream stalls for more than 45 seconds, it automatically retries up to 3 times with exponential backoff. Any partial content received is preserved and delivered to you, so your tokens are never wasted.
Yes. On iOS 26, the app uses native GlassView components for translucent, depth-aware UI elements. On older iOS versions, it gracefully falls back to BlurView effects. The app is designed to feel like a first-party iOS application.
The app itself is free. You only pay for the tokens you use through your own OpenAI API key. Costs depend on the model and reasoning effort level you choose. Check OpenAI's pricing page for current rates.

Complete Native Rewrite

Privacy

Your data stays yours

Liquid Glass Chat is designed with privacy as a core principle.

🛡️

Privacy Policy

  • ✓ No data collection. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your conversations, messages, or personal information to our servers.
  • ✓ Local storage only. All conversations and settings are stored locally on your device. Your API key is protected using iOS Secure Enclave (SecureStore).
  • ✓ Direct API communication. Your messages are sent directly from your device to OpenAI's API. There are no proxy servers or intermediaries.
  • ✓ No analytics or tracking. The app does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or user tracking mechanisms.
  • ✓ No account required. You do not need to create an account with us. The only credential needed is your own OpenAI API key.
  • ✓ OpenAI's policies apply. Your interactions with OpenAI's API are subject to OpenAI's Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
