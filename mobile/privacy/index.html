GlassGPT privacy

Privacy, compressed.

GlassGPT keeps the app local-first, but the content you submit can be processed by external AI providers.

What can be sent Back to mobile site

What can be sent

Only the content needed for the request you started.

Messages

The text you type and the model settings attached to that request.

Attachments

Images, documents, and files only when you choose to attach them.

Voice

Audio only when you record and send it.

Recovery

Response identifiers may be used to resume in-progress replies.

Who can receive it

Two providers may be involved, depending on your routing settings.

  • OpenAI processes requests needed to generate responses.
  • Cloudflare is only in the route when Gateway mode is enabled.

What stays local

The app keeps the working state on device unless you explicitly send it.

  • Your API key is stored in Keychain.
  • Conversation history, settings, and cached files stay local to the device.

Your controls

Privacy choices should stay visible and reversible.

  • Consent should be shown before first AI use.
  • Cloudflare Gateway is optional and can be disabled in Settings.
  • You can remove keys, clear chats, and clear cached files from the app.
